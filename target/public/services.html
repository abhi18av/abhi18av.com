Our Offerings

  • Software Development

    We love technology & and we surely love to code. Whether you need a custom solution for your business or you need someone to code your idea, which most definitely will become the next Twitter or Facebook, drop us a line or use the chat on this site to get in contact. We're looking forward to hearing from you.

  • Consulting

    Our team covers a broad palette of technologies as well as whole technology stacks and brings plenty of years of experience to the table. We offer onsite technology consulting. Whether you are about to start a green field project or try to bring change to an existing software solution, we can help you to move forward and meet your goals.

  • Training

    We not only love code, we also love to teach coding: "Code excellent and talk about it." You want your team hit the ground running with a new technology? Training is the way to go. We offer single- and multi-day training courses for a couple of technologies that will boost the productivity of your team. Let us know about your ideas and together we will figure something out that meet you needs.

  • Open Source Integration

    As an open source integrator we offer cost effective solutions built on top of the most proven open source technologies. Open Source is a treasure trove of well established solutions that can help you digitize and transform your business. Having not only released and contributed to a lot of open source projects, but also having studied open source scientifically we understand what makes open source tick and how to make it work for you.

  • Coaching

    Don't make the same mistakes we did! Take the shortcut, by letting us help you avoid the same traps. We have plenty of experience in software development and we like to share it. It's not only knowing your way around technology. It's also about how you think about it and how you do it. Whether as an individual or a small team coaching is probably the most effective way to acquire new skills and to learn and adopt a mindset that helps building better software.

  • Workshops

    Building a successful company is hard. Really hard. At 200ok, we have launched multiple incubators and spinned-off companies, so we have a little experience in how to optimize your chances of success. For that we have created multiple workshops: Traction: A startup guide to getting customers. Bullseye: The Bullseye framework is designed to be a straightforward way to direct your traction focus and maximize your results. Lean Startup: Be more innovative. Stop wasting people's time. Be more successful. Scrum: Scrum is an Agile framework for completing complex projects.

  • Hosting & Maintenance

    We support our clients and products in all areas of Unix/Linux infrastructure. 200ok works with true DevOps in mind where our developers are not just developers, but have a strong background in different flavors of Unix and Linux. All our applications are deployed through automated configuration management, many run within containers, all have automated monitoring and exception notification and either run in Swiss data centers on private networks or on any cloud service provider.

    We also offer to support and maintain the applications that we build at various levels to meet the clients' demands - from regular security updates to adding new functionality. In cooperation with our partners we are able to provide 24/7 on-call service.
